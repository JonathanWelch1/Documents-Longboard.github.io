The Cruise Hub

This is a site dedicated to showcasing the best long boarding hills on the K-State campus. As we find more, we will add them.

This hill is located on the east side of Hale Library. The Hale hill is an easy, slow hill. I recommend this hill for beginners or those looking for a nice gentle cruise. Rating: Beginner

The Bluemont hill is located on the south side of Bluemont. Bluemont hill is fairly straight with a long, gentle slop. A nice feature about this hill is traffic is one way, the same way you are going. Rating: Intermediate

The Anderson hill is located between Anderson and Fairchild. The Anderson hill is long and requires a turn at high speed. Rating: Difficult

Cardiac Hill is the most difficult hill currently on the site. It is located on the northeast corner of the quad. The hill starts with a steep incline followed by a double S shaped path. The most difficult part of this hill is the timing. Cardiac is a high traffic area and requires precision timing to successfully complete. Many people have taken bad spills because of traffic. Our boy did a sicc leg sweep on some pleb that didn't get out of the way. Rating: Expert
