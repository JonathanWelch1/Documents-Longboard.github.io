This hill is located on the east side of Hale Library. The Hale hill is a long hill with a gradual slope. I recommend this hill for beginners or those looking for a nice gentle cruise.

Rating: Beginner

On Map: Blue Trail

The Bluemont hill is located on the south side of Bluemont. Bluemont hill is fairly straight with a long, gentle slope. A nice feature about this hill is traffic is one-way in the direction you are riding.

Rating: Intermediate

On Map: Green Trail

The Anderson hill is located between Anderson and Fairchild Hall. The Anderson hill is long and requires a turn at high speed.

Rating: Difficult

On Map: Orange Trail

Cardiac Hill is the most difficult hill currently on the site. It is located on the northeast corner of the quad. The hill starts with a steep incline followed by a double S-shaped path. The most difficult part of this hill is the timing. Cardiac is a high traffic area and requires precision timing to successfully complete. Many people have taken bad spills because of traffic. Our boy did a sicc leg sweep on some pleb that didn't get out of the way.

Rating: Expert
